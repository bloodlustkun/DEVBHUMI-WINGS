# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## What is the booking policy?
We require a 25% advance payment to initiate your booking. The remaining balance is due after booking confirmation.

## How do I make a payment?
You can pay securely online. Details will be provided during the booking process.

## Can I cancel my booking?
Yes, please refer to our cancellation policy for details. Contact support for assistance.

## Is my data safe?
Yes, we value your privacy. Please see our Privacy Policy for more information.
